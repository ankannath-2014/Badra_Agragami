About us

Badra Agragami is a Non - Profit Organisation

We are team of hard working people dedicated towards the welfare of the people in the society. Established in 1973 and continuing with dedicataion and love for the people for the last 50 years and keep vision to continue further in future.

Durga Puja

Durga Puja is the biggest festival of our Country and indeed ours as well. We celebrate Durga PUja Every Year the Biggest Way possible. We had great years with great themes, idols, recognitions, awards and many more. But this year we celebrate the Golden jubilee of this great and Auspiciousfetival of yours. We plan to have it bigger, better and filled with a lot of surprises.

Social Work

We are involved in a lot of social activities throught the year. We organizes Blood Donation Camp, Cloth and Food Distribution for needy, Annual Picnics, and many more cultural events as well.

Annual Sports and Cultural Events

We also keeps engaging with lot of sports and cultural activities to keep your young generation fit and healthy and involved in a lot of activites such as different types of sports, Fitness training, Cricket, Football, Siniging, Dancing and lot more. We also Organise Annual sports in our own Badra Playing ground and Cultural Programmes as well.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Take a look at the Frequently Asked Questions to clear your doubts. More Queries? Then Please Feel Free to Contact With us.

  • How to Reach the Destination?

    Take a Auto from Aiport 1 No. Gate to Durganagar. Walking Distance From Railway Sation (Sealdah Bongaon Branch) . Take a ToTo or Walk from the Belgharia Express Way (Durganagar Bus Stop) .

  • What is the area coverage of the whole puja?

    Total Area Covered is 80,000 sq. feet. The Dimension of The puja Pandel Will be 200 feet x 200 feet x 80 feet .And a giant Screen will be allocated in the main program stage of 60 feet x 50 feet .

  • What is our Crowd Pulling Mechanism?

    Promotion includes social media promotion, youtube vlogging, banners, posters and many more.Branding includes collaborating with influencers and popular brands. Inauguration planning is done with big names in film industry and sports person.

  • What is our Crowd Holding Mechanism?

    Outside Pandel the crowd will be holded for 30 seconds for a brilliant laser lighting and sound show. Inside Pandel different figures and idol of devi durga will a holding time of 30 seconds.Security and Volunteers will be appointed and will be responsible for managing the crowd and maintain a peaceful Pandel Hopping.

  • What is the main USP of the Puja and How can we Sponsor or Donate to the Cause?

    This year we step into the Golden Jubilee of the Auspicious festival of Maktri Sakti Aradhana or the Durga Puja. The greatest attraction of this year is the Theme. The theme is kept a secret to protect our interest. The theme is never showcased in any of the puja pandels till date in all over kolkata with such intensity and revolutionary.

    For all Sponsors related queries and Donation please contact with the given contact Details. Feel free to ask for any query.All kind of instituions and Orginization are allowed to connect with us and make it even bigger and better.
